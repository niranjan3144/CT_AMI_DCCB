Ways of Working

  • 1
    Submit a single CIP/DCR via the Booking Tool. Additional documents go into the auto-created dedicated Teams channel.
  • 2
    Change Lead & Program Lead must conduct a Pre-Review with all DCCB members before the meeting.
  • 3
    All CFT members pre-review the CIP/DCR and complete impact assessments before the DCCB meeting. No open topics in the meeting.
  • 4
    DQE confirms completeness of CIP/DCR (including GDP). Then update Work Tracker: "Ready for CRB".
  • 5
    Incomplete forms are sent back or taken last — decided by DCCB Chair.
  • 6
    Always present from the Live Shared Copy in the Teams channel. Offline copies are not allowed.
  • 7
    CR Number must be created in Windchill before the DCCB. Route CR within 5 working days post-DCCB.
  • 8
    Recommended changes from DCCB → email to all members for comments before routing in Windchill.
  • 9
    RA Impact TAT: 1 day for simple changes, 3 days for complex (Refresh, CAPA, etc.).
⚠️
Everyone must request access to Teams/SharePoint once before joining. Use the Booking Link to submit DCCB requests — it auto-creates a dedicated Teams channel with your CR# as title.
📌
Attend only if the change pertains to your product. Agenda shared via Teams Chat/E-mail as a reminder.
